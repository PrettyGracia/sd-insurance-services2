Public and Products Liability Insurance

SD Insurance's Public and Products Liability Insurance is a crucial coverage for businesses of all sizes. This insurance protects your business against legal liability for personal injury or property damage caused to third parties as a result of your business activities or products. Whether you operate a small business or a large enterprise, our Public and Products Liability Insurance provides the peace of mind you need to focus on growing your business.

Key Features of Public and Products Liability Insurance

  • Comprehensive Coverage: Protection against claims for personal injury or property damage caused by your business operations or products.
  • Legal Defense Costs: Coverage for legal defense costs associated with defending claims made against your business.
  • Third-Party Property Damage: Protection against claims for damage to third-party property caused by your business activities.
  • Product Liability: Coverage for claims arising from defects in products manufactured, sold, or supplied by your business.
  • Worldwide Coverage: Protection for your business activities and products, regardless of where they are conducted or sold.
  • Flexible Limits: Choose from a range of coverage limits to suit your business needs and budget.
